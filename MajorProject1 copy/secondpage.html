The Mountain

Gaining elevation, I roll down my window to feel a crisp breeze of wind. The forced gasp of chills allows my body to relax. The numbing sensation was appropriate for the stress aquired prior. I slowly make my way throughout the plowed roads. The flatirons sit high in an eternal chair surrounded by the snowcapped trees. I bring my car to a slow halt curiously noticing trails. The moment feels so surreal What should I do?
