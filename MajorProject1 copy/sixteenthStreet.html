Downtown 16th Street

The 16th street mall is filled with people endlessly moving about their days. I curiously bring myself through glass-walled skyscrapers and historic buildings. The street is lined with trees offering a natural tie to the urban design. Following my curiousity, I embrace the diverse culture surrounding and could not help but notice the amount of streetperformers who filled the city with harmonic tunes and happiness. As I journey through the downtown, I feel the mountain breeze whirl through the towers changing direction at each block. Buses, Bikes, and fancy cars all pass through creating a non-stop sound of horns, engines, and brakes. Each street holds a different story and I could get lost in them. Let's go somewhere else!
