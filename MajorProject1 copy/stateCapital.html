Colorado State Capitol

The Capitol Building sits above the rest of the city with a distinctive gold dome. The detailed architecture gives a neoclassical feel to its design furthermore holding significance to its historic ties. Influenced by American history its hard not to notice the stained glass capturing each ray of sunlight. It's most interesting to notice that on the fifteenth step of the entrance marks the words "One Mile Above Sea Level" signifying the city's unusual location. All the material in the building come from surrounding Colorado areas which includes marble, pink stone, granite, etc. The gold dome was gifted by the local Colorado Gold Miners furthermore attributing the important events which occured here long ago.
